# AI-provider data-handling contract checklist
*Owner: Chapter 15. For every external model or connector provider.*

- [ ] Zero-data-retention (or equivalent) terms in writing
- [ ] No training on your data, covering the default tier and any "improvement" or "feedback" programmes
- [ ] Abuse-monitoring / human-review opt-out (or a named, contained disclosure path where a full opt-out is unavailable)
- [ ] Retention windows and deletion SLAs, with evidence of deletion
- [ ] Sub-processor list & change notice
- [ ] Change-control and deprecation notice for model versions and connector behaviour
- [ ] Data residency / sovereignty commitments (reconcile with Chapter 2)
- [ ] Security & resilience commitments
- [ ] Audit & information rights, including pooled / third-party audit where direct audit is refused
- [ ] Incident notification terms (feed your IR, Chapter 12)
- [ ] Exit / portability & deletion on termination
- [ ] Concentration-risk view if provider is systemic (Chapter 15)
- [ ] Confirm the runtime tier/configuration matches the contracted terms, not the self-serve default (Chapter 15)

> *Template, provided "as is", not legal or compliance advice. Adapt to your environment and obligations; the owning chapter in the book governs.*
