# AI-provider data-handling contract checklist
*Owner: C15-S1. For every external model or connector provider.*

- [ ] Zero-data-retention (or equivalent) terms in writing
- [ ] No training on your data without explicit opt-in
- [ ] Sub-processor list & change notice
- [ ] Data residency / sovereignty commitments (reconcile with C02-S1)
- [ ] Security & resilience commitments; right to audit / evidence
- [ ] Incident notification terms (feed your IR — C12)
- [ ] Exit / portability & deletion on termination
- [ ] Concentration-risk view if provider is systemic (C15-S1)

> *Template, provided "as is", not legal or compliance advice. Adapt to your environment and obligations; the owning chapter in the book governs.*
