# Checklist 4 — Governance and assurance (Part V)

Run this at the governance forum that owns AI risk. It closes the loop from operating model to board reporting.

- [ ] The **AI governance operating model** assigns ownership, decision rights, and escalation for AI risk — owner **Chapter 13**.
- [ ] Each AI use case carries a **firm-internal AI risk tier** that drives control depth — owner **Chapter 13**.
- [ ] Generative and agentic systems brought under **model risk management as a control**, adapted for genAI — owner **Chapter 14**.
- [ ] **Third-party AI governance** covers onboarding, monitoring, and concentration risk — owner **Chapter 15**.
- [ ] AI risk **quantified with FAIR** in loss terms the board can act on — owner **Chapter 16**.
- [ ] An **assurance / evidence map** ties each control to operating evidence — owner **Chapter 17**.
- [ ] Control effectiveness read against the **master control-by-regime crosswalk** — owner **Chapter 18**.

> *Practitioner aid, not a compliance position. Each item names its **owning section** in the book;
> where a line and its owning chapter diverge, the chapter governs.*
